WHAT IS AN EASY ACCESS PASS?

The Easy Access Pass is a special pass in the park which supports guests that require any additional needs throughout their stay at the resort. The Easy Access Pass is a way we ensure all of our wanted customers enjoy the same experience as anyone else, regardless if there are requirements or not.
The Pass system allows guests to line up virtually for rides with up to two other guests (can be non Easy Access pass holders and must have one 18+)
NOTE: having a disability does not automatically mean you are entitled to a Easy Access Pass. All applicants must go through the Pass process in order to receive your pass

WHO IS ELIGIBLE FOR THE PASS?

eligibility for the pass is considered and reviewed by our dedicated team for special passes.

You are eligible if:
- You are currently under the Blue Badge scheme
- You are entitled to free travel via a travel pass
- You have mobility difficulties ( doesnt have to be solely wheelchair users)

note: applicants do not need to strictly follow these requirements as they are individually checked upon and applicants may be accepted even if they do not meet full requirements.

how do i apply for the pass?

in order to obtain an easy access pass, you will need to contact our special pass teeam via:

phone: 0800300400
email: easypass@westeros.co.uk

please be prepared to give us an image of a valid ID stating DOB, full name and address.

how to use the pass

using your pass is very simple!

once accepted, you will reeive the pass via postal service or via email (depending on how you requested).

when you arrive at the park, you will need to show your pass to the central hub next to the entrance with a valid ID with residency of the same place for at least 2 years. we will then give you a special day pass.

when obtained, the pass will now be your entry to all rides and events at the theme park via virtual qeues.
this is done by showing the ride/ event manager your id and allowing them to scan the barcode on the pass.

now all you have to do is relax and enjoy the ride!

remember! you can add 2 other people onto the virtual waiting list to go with you!
